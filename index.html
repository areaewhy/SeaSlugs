The Sea Slugs are a small dance orchestra that specializes in playing for family dances, which are taught by an expert caller.

The music performed on fiddles, accordions, flute, guitar, mandolin, and string bass, is deeply rooted in styles popular in southern Maine for generations: Franco-American, reels, Irish jigs, and early Anglo-American dance music. Our caller teaches contrdances, waltzes, polkas, and even an occasional tango to dancers of all levels of experience.

The Slugs have been performing steadily throughout southern Maine for twenty years. The group is ideal for a wide range of public and private events, including family gatherings, public events and corporate functions. Several of the slugs are teachers and the group is skilled at conducting school workshops in folk dancing and music performance.
